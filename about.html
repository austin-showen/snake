About SnakeShip

Pilot your ship to collect as much energy as possible.

Use the arrow keys, WASD, or the onscreen arrow buttons to change your ship's direction.

The game ends if you hit a wall, an asteroid, or your own exhaust trail.

Watch out! The more energy you collect, the faster you'll be!

Created by Austin Showen in May 2023. Part of the Software Engineering Immersive program at General Assembly.

I adapted game sprites and audio from the following users on opengameart.org:

  • Kenney.nl - Sound effects
  • dsonyy - Spaceship sprite
  • Funwithpixels - Asteroid sprite
  • CodeManu - Energy sprite
  • trezegames - Mute, pause, and reset icons

I created the remaining assets on Piskel, an open-source sprite editor.

Background image by Andy Holmes.
